Artist Statement

My name is Sargylana Cherepanova.
    In Sakha language my name means "happiness". I'm a game designer and an interdisciplinary Sakha artist that creates videogames as a form of art.

Sakha people are Indigenous to Sakha Republic, located in North-East-Siberian part of russia.

             

             

              My art practice is mainly focused on creating videogame prototypes with the decolonial focus in various game engines..

             

      I dream that Sakha Republic could become free.
