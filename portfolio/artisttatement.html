Artist Statement
My name is Sargylana Cherepanova. In Sakha language my name means "happiness". I'm a game designer and an interdisciplinary Sakha artist that creates videogames as a form of art. Sakha people are Indigenous to Sakha Republic. I want to decolonize videogame space as seen from a perspective of Indigenous Sakha person. I dream that Sakha Republic could be free
